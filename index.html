Anonymous

We know anonymity is important. For those moments when it matters most, you can choose to post anonymously to confess, seek advice, or get something off your chest—or reply anonymously to share an honest, genuine perspective.

Engaging Conversations

No more creating a separate post to respond anonymously. With thread, every post and its replies stay connected–even anonymous ones. Follow stories, contribute, and never lose track. Conversations are better when they stay organized.

Open Yet Exclusive

Anyone can participate in any campus, but verified users stand out with the badge proving they're real students or alumni—even on anonymous posts. This duality enables open participation while ensuring relevance for you: filter posts to see only from verified members of the campus.

Share as Photo

Turn any post into a photo with just one tap—no more taking multiple screenshots. Share it with friends, save it to your phone, or post it to other social media platform.

Explore Campuses

Everyone is here—students you might never meet in person. Explore posts, confessions, and discussions beyond your campus. Every campus has a story for you to discover.

Content Creation

Choose from Text, Media, or Link mode to creatively craft your post—whether it's a heartfelt confession, a captivating photo, or a link you want to share. Flexible. Simple. Powerful.

Native App Design

Seamless and intuitive design that just works—and feels right. From its fast performance to its clean layout, the app delivers an experience that feels amazing to use.

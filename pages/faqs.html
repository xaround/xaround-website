Frequently Asked Questions

Updated July 9, 2025

Communities & Roles

Campus communities are official groups for universities and schools. Circle communities revolve around shared hobbies, interests, or topics.

Go to the Communities tab, pick a group you're interested in, and tap "Follow." Posts from communities you follow will appear in your home feed.

Roles highlight your status within each community, such as "Verified Student." Roles are specific to each community you join.

Community moderators handle verification. Check each community's page for their specific verification process, which might involve student email verification or other criteria.

Creating & Managing Content

Tap the create button, select your community, and start writing. You can add text, one image or GIF, one link with a preview, hashtags, and a flair.

Toggle "Post Anonymously" when creating your post. Your username won't appear, but any verification badge remains visible to maintain trust.

You can delete your posts and replies anytime. Editing isn't available yet. Anonymous posts can't be edited or deleted since they aren't linked to your account.

Privacy & Safety

Yes. Your username isn't stored with the content, and no one, including our team, can link it back to you. All content still goes through moderation.

Tap "..." on any post or reply, select "Report," choose the appropriate violation, and add any details if needed.

Currently, you can hide posts or report users. Full blocking features are coming soon.

Technical Support

Android 11.0 (API 30) or higher, iOS 15.0 or higher.

Make sure your app is updated, restart your device, reinstall the app, or contact support with device details if the issue persists.

Account Management

Email us at nikfikrics@gmail.com from your registered address, including your username. We'll process it within 30 days. Deletion is permanent; anonymous posts remain.

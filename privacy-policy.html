Privacy Policy

Updated July 9, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how xaround collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Sign-in credentials (Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In)
  • Email address (for account verification and communication)
  • Username and profile information you provide

Content and Usage Information

  • Posts, comments, and other content you create
  • Community interactions and engagement data
  • App usage patterns and feature utilization

Device and Technical Information

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • IP address and general location (for security and community matching)
  • Crash reports and performance data

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve our app services
  • Enable community features and content discovery
  • Ensure platform safety through content moderation
  • Communicate important updates and support
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience

Anonymous Posting

When you post anonymously, your username is not stored with the content. Anonymous posts cannot be linked back to your account by anyone, including our team. However, all content still undergoes our standard moderation processes.

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect safety and prevent abuse
  • With service providers who help operate our app (under strict confidentiality)

Data Retention

  • Account information: Until account deletion
  • Regular posts: Removed when you delete your account
  • Anonymous posts: Retained but cannot be linked to you
  • Technical data: Up to 2 years for app improvement

Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and update your account information
  • Delete your posts and account
  • Control app permissions through device settings
  • Request information about data we have collected

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no internet transmission is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

xaround is not intended for users under 17 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 17. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

International Users

xaround is operated from Malaysia. By using our app, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in Malaysia, which may have different data protection laws than your country.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be announced through the app. Your continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, contact me at nikfikrics@gmail.com.

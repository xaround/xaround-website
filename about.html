About

Reimagining Campus Community Conversations

Campus communities deserve better than makeshift solutions. Today, most rely on volunteer-run Facebook Pages or Telegram Channels coupled with Google Forms for anonymous submissions—creating fragmented, unreliable experiences that fail to nurture real connections.

Current Reality

Campus communities face significant challenges with existing platforms:

  1. Continuity Crisis: When volunteer administrators graduate or move on, entire community spaces often disappear, erasing valuable knowledge and breaking community bonds.
  2. Participation Barriers: Without proper privacy controls for anonymous engagement, many community members hesitate to join important conversations or share valuable perspectives.
  3. Conversation Chaos: Unstructured threads and limited organization tools make following and participating in anonymous discussions unnecessarily difficult.
  4. Frustrating Delays: Manual review process for Google Forms submissions creates lengthy waits between questions and responses, stifling real-time community interaction.

Introducing xaround

Built exclusively for campuses, xaround unites these fragmented experiences into a cohesive platform where communities thrive:

  1. Institutional Memory: Communities persist beyond individual administrators, preserving knowledge and maintaining continuity.
  2. Thoughtful Anonymity: AI-powered moderation and merit-based reply system ensures quality conversations while protecting privacy when needed.
  3. Structured Engagement: Intuitive threading and organization makes following discussions natural and engaging, whether anonymous or identified.
  4. Immediate Interaction: Post instantly, with AI moderator working behind the scenes 24/7 to maintain community standards.

xaround isn't just another social app—it's purpose-built infrastructure for the unique dynamics of campus communities, creating spaces where meaningful conversation and connection flourish.
